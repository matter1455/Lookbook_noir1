Film Noir: The Cost of Living

Vision and Concept

Unifying idea/main message: The purpose of the play, to us at least, is that of transaction or money in society. For example, on page 127, John offers to “pay” (Majok 127) Jess “for the whole” (Majok 127) day: essentially to John this relationship was completely based on employer and employee. Additionally, for Eddie and Ani’s relationship it's similar: they talk about the insurance plan in which Ani says “I’m still on yer plan” (Majok 40). The reason for meeting is based on Eddie gathering his stuff or insurance. So in practicality, we believe that the play should be experienced in both a realistic and capitalistic way: it should fully embody what the 1940s felt like, while still maintaining the themes of relationships and money.

Money is the Cost Of Living: the fabricator of transactional, fraudulent, and true relationships.
